Common Questions

Everything you need to know about placing an errand with Foodoor.

We currently operate exclusively in Gwarimpa, Abuja. Both your pickup and delivery locations must be within Gwarimpa for us to serve you at this time. We are actively working to expand to other parts of Abuja very soon.
Currently you can pay on delivery (cash) or via bank transfer. Payment details will be shared with you via WhatsApp when your order is confirmed. We are working on in-app wallet payment for future versions.
If no rider accepts within 5 minutes, you'll be prompted to increase your service fee to attract more riders. After 3 retries with no acceptance, your order will be cancelled at no charge to you.
Yes. You can cancel for free at any time before a rider accepts your request. If you cancel after a rider has accepted, a 50% service fee applies. Once the rider has already picked up your item, no refund is available.
